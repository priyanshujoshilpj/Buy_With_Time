About us

Great thinkers have a spectacular time to be more valuable currency than money. With this objective we have designed an online networking platform, Buy With Time !, which allows customers to pay for goods with their time. The coins will be assigned to the customers according to how much time they have spent traveling to the stores which are a bit outside the city areas. The travel timeline and the directions followed for arriving at the stores are traced out by Google Maps.

Read more


How The Process Works ?

  • 01

    Open Google Maps

    User will open google maps and will start navigating to our store from his live location.

  • 02

    Calculate The Distance

    Once the user reaches the store he has to show the distance calculated by google maps to the cashier.

  • 03

    Calculate Coins

    Now the user will be assigned coins corresponding to the distance travelled according to the rule : 1 coin = 100 metre

  • 04

    Purchase the Products

    Now the user can purchase the products and Every product would have specific coins assigned as well as an MRP price. Customers can also buy products by paying half by coins gained and another part by cash.
